Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Catalog Match. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website or use our desktop application.

2. Data Collection

Catalog Match is a desktop application that processes data locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or transmit your product images or catalog data to our servers. All processing happens offline on your device.

3. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the application, such as crash reports or feature usage metrics. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@anchored.site.
